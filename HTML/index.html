Shippers

SAFE Transportation works with customers across the country in all types of industries. Whether you run flatbeds, vans or temperature controlled equipment, we can match your company with the freight that best fits your drivers' needs. We offer freight options throughout the United States, Canada and Mexico as well as cross border throughout North America.

We understand cash flow is critical to your success. We ensure you get paid fast so you can keep your trucks on the road. We offer a “Next Day Quick Pay” which you can request when booking your load. We also have a “Standard Pay” which is 30 days upon receipt of your invoice.
