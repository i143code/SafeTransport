Shippers

SAFE Transportation is a non-asset based freight brokerage company. We are based in Blue Ash, OH and have nearly 30 years of experience brokering freight. Our capacity to handle your freight is limitless. We can find a truck or fill in on a difficult lane in a moment’s notice. We have over 30,000 insured carriers that are contracted with us to move your freight. We are never limited by fleet size, equipment, or pre-determined shipping routes. Although we were initially founded as an LTL brokerage firm, today we specialize in securing dry vans, reefers, flatbeds, box trucks and intermodal services. Your freight is our priority. We understand that every load is unique so we make sure to fully understand all your shipping parameters to ensure that you receive the best service at the lowest rate. Our attention to detail, from pickup thru delivery, is what allows us to avoid missed shipments, delays, and product shortages for our shippers.

Carriers

SAFE Transportation works with customers across the country in all types of industries. Whether you run flatbeds, vans or temperature controlled equipment, we can match your company with the freight that best fits your drivers' needs. We offer freight options throughout the United States and Canada.

We understand cash flow is critical to your success. We ensure you get paid fast so you can keep your trucks on the road. We offer a “Next Day Quick Pay” which you can request when booking your load or you can use our “Standard Pay” which is 30 days upon receipt of your invoice.

About Us

Mission:

SAFE Transportation partners with manufacturers to provide cost-effective transportation solutions to move the nation’s freight on-time and without damage – every time. Our team of industry experts tracks shipments from pick-up thru delivery and provides information to all stakeholders along the way.

Vision:

SAFE Transportation is the nation’s most reliable freight brokerage firm. We provide companies an alternative to the larger, less organized freight brokerage firms. With over 25 years of freight brokerage experience, SAFE continues to grow organically through satisfied customer referrals.
